    Minimum System Requirements

    Minimum Specifications

    • CPU: Dual-core processor, 2.0 GHz
    • RAM: 2 GB
    • Disk Space: 500 MB
    • Network: 100 Mbps

    Recommended Specifications

    Recommended for Production

    • CPU: Quad-core processor, 3.0+ GHz
    • RAM: 8 GB
    • Disk Space: 2 GB
    • Network: 1 Gbps
